Work Showcase

RYUICHI SAKAMOTO

1957

Merry Christmas Mr. Lawrence

Sakamoto's first major film score, which includes the hauntingly beautiful main theme. This soundtrack became an instant classic and remains one of his most recognized works.

1987

The Last Emperor

Co-composed with David Byrne and Cong Su, this Oscar-winning score blends Asian and Western musical elements, creating an evocative soundscape that enhances the epic story of the last emperor of China.

2017

Async

A deeply personal and experimental album, async reflects Sakamoto’s contemplations on life, mortality, and nature, influenced by his battle with cancer. The album is a blend of ambient, classical, and minimalistic elements. async is celebrated as one of his most profound works, showing his introspective and experimental side.
