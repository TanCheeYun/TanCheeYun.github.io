Animenz

Works Showcase

Sony Music Labels Inc. (2014)

Unravel

Animenz's piano arrangement of "Unravel" marks an important milestone in his career. This piece not only shows his love for anime music but also gained him worldwide recognition. As the theme song of the popular anime Tokyo Ghoul, "Unravel" captures the character's inner struggle and loneliness. Animenz brings these emotions to life through his piano adaptation, making the piece deeply moving. This arrangement introduced many to the beauty of anime music and had a lasting impact on his career.

Media Factory (2014)

This Game

Animenz's piano arrangement of "This Game," the opening theme of No Game No Life, showcases his talent for transforming anime music into expressive solo performances. With its fast-paced, intense melody, "This Game" captures the thrill and strategy of the anime's world. Through complex techniques and dynamic shifts, Animenz brings out the energy and determination of the characters, creating a powerful and captivating piece that resonates with anime fans worldwide.

Key Sounds Label (2010)

My Soul, Your Beats!

Animenz's piano arrangement of "My Soul, Your Beats!", the opening theme of Angel Beats!, captures the emotional depth of the original song. With its poignant melody, this piece reflects the themes of hope and longing in the anime. Animenz brings these emotions to life, creating a moving experience that resonates with fans and highlights his skill in expressing complex feelings through piano.
